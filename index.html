About The Inn On The Lake

In 15 acres of grounds with lawns sweeping to the beaches of Ullswater, this award-winning four-star hotel in Glenridding offers one of the most stunning sites in the Lake District. The Inn on the Lake Ullswater boasts 48 en-suite rooms, most of which have breathtaking views of Lake Ullswater or the spectacular Helvellyn mountain range. Experience our 8 Luxury Collection bedrooms, the most coveted rooms in our Lake District hotel. These gorgeous 4-star standard guest rooms are the most desirable accommodations in this Lake District hotel.

Enjoy morning coffee, light lunches, and afternoon teas in the Lake View lounges or the one-of-a-kind Orangery with 360-degree views of the lake and adjacent fells. Lake View Restaurant, which has two AA rosette awards, has something for everyone's taste, or for a more casual eating experience. The Ramblers Bar, located inside the grounds of this Ullswater hotel, serves outstanding traditional pub fare.

Originally owned by the Darnely family, the building incorporated a boating lake and swimming pool, and was leased on various occasions throughout the 1930s and 1940s. During this time, it was known as 'The Laughing Water Restaurant and Tea Rooms'.

The Inn on the Lake has a long and varied history dating back to 1830. Today, we present you with modern facilities, beautiful furnishings and high quality service within one of the most spectacular settings in the Lake District, on the shore of Lake Ullswater.
